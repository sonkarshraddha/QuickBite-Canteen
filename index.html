Ten Years of our Canteen

Ten years. TEN YEARS! Some days, it feels like we've been at this for a hundred. Other days, it feels like we're novices wondering how we got here. This decade has been everything — exhilarating, maddening, joyous.

We've served you plates we're insanely proud of, and meals that are still the talk of the campus. But let's be real: it also hasn't been all smooth sailing. For every "OMG-this-is-amazing" moment, there were about ten "What the hell were we thinking?!" ones.

Here's to ten years of flavour, failure, and fabulous memories — and to the laughter that made it all worth it.

"From food that flopped to ideas that fizzled, from students who became regulars to those who ended up on our 'never again' list — we've cherished every hit and every miss. All of it led to the creation of something that's now part of this college's soul."

— Canteen Staff
